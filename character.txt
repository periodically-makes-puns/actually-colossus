Prosecutor Markov Pierley 

Prosecutor Matthew Pierley (hereafter referred to as "Pierley"), is a a sort of odd character. Little to none is known about their backstory.

Backstory (known):
Pierley was born in an obscure village in the northern mountains, to an unknown family. He claims that his parent(s) left him to pursue their own ventures, but no corroborating evidence was ever found. At a young age, he moved to a nearby country (name not given), earning meagre wages in order to pay for his schooling. Eventually, he became a public prosecutor after graduating law school. 
His career is rather well-known; However, their personal life is shrouded in mystery.
